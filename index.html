I'm a front-end developer based in Chesapeake, VA, with a decade of digital marketing behind me. I've spent years on the business side — managing up to $80K/month in ad spend, building high-converting funnels, and taking eCommerce brands from $10K to $300K per month. I've led marketing at the National Hockey League, American Media Inc., and ran my own full-service digital agency. Now I'm building the interfaces myself.

That background gives me something rare at this level: I understand what users need to see, what makes them convert, and what a business actually cares about. I don't just write clean code — I write code that performs. Every project I build starts with one question: what does the user need to do here, and how do I make it obvious?

I hold a BS in Business from Ithaca College and I'm looking for a team where I can grow technically fast, contribute from day one, and build something that genuinely moves the needle.
